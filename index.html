Our Story

Blending Tradition with
Modern Sophistication

Since our inception, Surya Putra has been dedicated to the art of fine textiles. We believe that a piece of fabric is more than just material; it's a canvas of culture, a story of patience, and a legacy of excellence.

Our master artisans utilize heritage techniques, from the intricate wax-resist dyeing of Batik to the delicate weaving of imperial silk, ensuring every meter produced meets the highest standards of luxury.
